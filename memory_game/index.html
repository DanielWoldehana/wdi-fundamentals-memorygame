About

This Game presents the player with different Memory retaining challanges that prove difficult as the gamer moves to higher levels in the game. While it is fun to try and identify similar pictures and and signs it is also a great to train the gamers short and long term memory which in return will assist you in your day to day life.

Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
